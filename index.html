North Adah Kay
In the beginning there was a young boy. He liked all the regular young boy stuff: cooking, karate, sewing, archery, nail polish, robots, & video games. He got along ok with other kids, but never very well. Life was fine for a while, & it went on.
One day, everything changed. At theatre camp, a friend complimented his incredibly basic tee shirt, setting something off inside the young North. “It’s such an incredibly basic shirt!” North ranted. “There is nothing interesting about it! No interesting seams, no interesting collar, no interesting fabric. Ugh, boy clothes are so dumb. Boys barely have to do anything to be considered to look ‘nice’.”
(side note: North is still bad at taking compliments)
Now, North wears basically whatever she wants. She grew out her pretty, wavy hair & got cool tattoos. Sometimes she wears boy clothes (they are generally easier to wash & more durable for manual labor) & mostly she wears girl clothes (once she started wearing girl pants, she just couldn’t go back to the miserable straight cut of men’s pants).
